Question Only Test

In this test, you will need to answer 30 random questions which only consists of lebanese law and driving knowledge. You will not be asked questions regarding lebanese driving signs meaning. In order to pass this test, you must score above 24.

Signs Only Test

In this test, you will need to answer 30 questions which only consists of driving signs and their meaning. You will not be asked questions regarding lebanese driving law and knowledge. In order to pass this test, you must score above 25.

Start Signs Test

Full Test

This is the full test. You will be asked 20 random questions that are related to lebanese driving law and knowledge, in addition to 10 random questions regarding driving signs. In order to pass this test, you will need to score above 24. Good Luck

Things To Keep In Mind

Here are some things to keep in mind when taking the driving test:

  • Do no overthink the questions
  • Read the questions one night before the exam.
  • The day of the exam, make sure you take a good breakfast. This is very important to keep your concentration when taking the exam
  • The exam is not hard, but you need to know every question since some of them are not trivial, especially questions related to the law.
